Try JStock Android

JStock Android can be run under Android. Just click on Google Play icon to go to JStock Android page. If your can't access Google Play, you may download jstock-android.apk and install it manually.

Download JStock

JStock can be run under Windows, Linux, Mac (OS X). The current stable release is version 1.0.7.10. Just click on your operating system icon to download JStock.
